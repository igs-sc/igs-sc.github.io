About IGS-SC

The Interdisciplinary Graduate School Student Club (IGS–SC) is the official student club, supported by the IGS and all the institutions under its umbrella, and affiliated to NTU Graduate Student Council. One of the most active and dynamic student clubs of NTU, IGS-SC began its activities in January 2013 with a handful of members and has been steadily growing, both in number and activity. The club works towards strengthening the interpersonal and professional interaction among the IGS students, to enrich their experiences during PhD and beyond.
