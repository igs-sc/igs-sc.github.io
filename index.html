Our Events

Let's Get Together and have Fun!

  • Recreational

    What would university life be, without having fun? We organize various events to spice up your graduate life as an IGS student. From outings to the cinema to watch the latest movies (with popcorn included), to paintball where you get to finally show your appreciation to your fellow students and maybe even the Dean... We got you covered: fun, food and drinks, that is how we roll!

  • Academic

    Learning happens anytime and everywhere, so interdisciplinary students are encouraged to be versatile scholars with multiple skillsets and areas of expertise/familiarity. To this purpose, we arrange different workshops to enhance your skills (i.e. time-management, presentations) and organise industrial visits to established labs such as MITRE, to widen your horizons. We want you to take the most of this unique IGS experience.

  • Welfare

    We at IGS-SC believe in the philosophy of sharing joy and giving back to the society by regularly encouraging our students to look beyond their often busy and sometimes privileged lifestyles. We organize a myriad of welfare events, be it recreational outings with the differently abled or distribution of welfare packs to the elderly, both independently and/or in collaboration with the NTU Welfare Society.

  • Sports

    Life is too short to be lethargic and unathletic and to hold firm to this view we at IGS-SC organize and participate in a plethora of events throughout the year. Be it any interschool games, open leagues or the coveted IGS Open Games, we have a radiant presence in all. Our highly decorated football team brought several laurels last season, which includes winning the NTU Open Leagues Championship, and a silver each in the Interschool Games and IGS open games.
